Jessica Henwick

Actress | Writer | Producer
Official Photos »
Jessica Henwick is an English actress. She is best known for her roles as Nymeria Sand in the HBO series Game of Thrones (2011), and Colleen Wing in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, making her debut in the Netflix television series Iron Fist. Her film debut was St Trinian's 2: The Legend of Fritton's Gold (2009) (2009). See full bio »

Born:

August 30, 1992 in England, UK
